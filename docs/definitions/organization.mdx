## Definition

A durable collective of people, software agents, or both that can act as a unit.

- **Boundaries:** Not a single person or software agent. Legal incorporation is not required, but a legal company is always an Organization. A branded initiative, product line, or program is usually a Concept unless it has its own durable collective agency. Not a query-defined group.
- **Hex Code:** `11`
- **Standard Alignment:** [`org:Organization`](https://www.w3.org/ns/org#Organization) (Exact)
