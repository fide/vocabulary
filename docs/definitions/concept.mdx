## Definition

A socially or formally recognized unit of meaning, such as a term, topic, category, label, initiative, or other abstract idea.

- **Boundaries:** Not a plain raw value. Use this for labels, brands, programs, or initiatives that do not have their own durable collective agency.
- **Hex Code:** `31`
- **Standard Alignment:** [`skos:Concept`](https://www.w3.org/2004/02/skos/core#Concept) (Close) + [`schema:DefinedTerm`](https://schema.org/DefinedTerm) (Close)
